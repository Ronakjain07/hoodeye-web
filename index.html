Welcome to Hoodeye, where your safety is our priority. Hoodeye is an innovative application dedicated to empowering users with crucial insights into their neighborhood's safety. Leveraging officially verified crime data provided by government organizations, Hoodeye ensures a comprehensive view of the safety profile of your intended destinations.


Our Mission :

At Hoodeye, our mission is to provide you with the tools and information needed to make informed decisions about your safety and well-being. We understand the importance of knowing the safety history of your neighborhood, especially when choosing a place to call home.

Why Hoodeye for Neighborhood Safety?

Live Location Insights:
Hoodeye utilizes advanced technology to detect your location in real time, providing instant safety insights for both familiar and new areas. Stay informed about your neighborhood's security status, whether you're at home or on the go.
Past Crime Data for Informed Choices:
Wondering about the safety history of your district? Hoodeye delivers a comprehensive overview of past crimes in the area where you reside. This information empowers you to assess the safety of your house location and make choices that align with your security priorities.
Safety Assessment for Residential Decisions:
Planning a move? Hoodeye assists you in evaluating the safety of a neighborhood before making significant residential decisions. We believe that everyone deserves the right to choose a home in a secure environment, and our safety assessments help you achieve just that.
Panic Button for Immediate Assistance:
Your safety is our utmost concern. Hoodeye features a panic button that, when pressed, instantly shares your location and provides details of nearby police stations. This ensures swift assistance in case you witness or experience any sort of crime, giving you the support you need when it matters most.
