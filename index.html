About Us

Welcome to Hoodeye, where your safety is our priority. Hoodeye is an innovative application dedicated to empowering users with crucial insights into their neighborhood's safety. Leveraging officially verified crime data provided by government organizations, Hoodeye ensures a comprehensive view of the safety profile of your intended destinations.


Our Mission :

At Hoodeye, our mission is to provide you with the tools and information needed to make informed decisions about your safety and well-being. We understand the importance of knowing the safety history of your neighborhood, especially when choosing a place to call home.

Why Hoodeye for Neighborhood Safety?

Live Location Insights:
Hoodeye employs advanced technology for real-time location detection, delivering instant safety insights for both familiar and new areas, ensuring security awareness at home or on the go.

Past Crime Data for Informed Choices:
Hoodeye provides a detailed history of past crimes in your district, empowering you to assess your home's safety and make informed security decisions.

Safety Assessment for Residential Decisions:
Hoodeye helps assess neighborhood safety for informed residential decisions, ensuring everyone can choose a secure home environment.

Panic Button for Immediate Assistance:
Hoodeye prioritizes your safety with a panic button, instantly sharing your location and nearby police details for swift assistance in case of any crime, ensuring support when you need it most.
